We are moving beyond "Text-to-Image" to **"State-to-Optimized-State"**.

*   **Investor Value**: AI that doesn't just *draw* pictures, but *fixes* reality.
*   **Capability**: A Digital Twin that hallucinates the optimal future, which the physical machine then pays to actualize.

### 4. Autonomous M2M Economies (The "Self-Healing" Grid)
Our **Sovereign DACs** utilize **AI Ripple** to settle transactions instantly between machines.
*   **Investor Value**: Unlocks the $20T+ Machine Economy. Machines can pay for their own maintenance, trade energy, and purchase optimization services without human friction.
