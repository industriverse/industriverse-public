## The Thesis
Traditional AI optimizes for *accuracy*. Thermodynamic AI optimizes for *energy*.
In an industrial setting, energy is the fundamental currency. By minimizing entropy (disorder) in physical systems, we maximize economic value.

## The Core Engine: TGE & Entropy Oracle

### 1. Thermodynamic Generative Executor (TGE)
The TGE does not just "generate code"; it generates *low-entropy physical actions*.
*   **Input**: A manufacturing goal (e.g., "Weld this seam").
*   **Process**: Simulates thousands of toolpaths, calculating the thermodynamic cost (heat, vibration, wear) of each.
*   **Output**: The single path of least resistance (The "Coolest Path").

### 2. The Entropy Oracle
A proprietary physics engine that monitors the "State of Order" in real-time.
*   **Metric**: Negentropy (J/K).
*   **Value**: Every Joule of energy saved is profit. Every reduction in thermal variance is quality.

## The Value Proposition
*   **For CFOs**: Lower energy bills (Negawatt Model).
*   **For Engineers**: Higher precision, less wear.
*   **For The World**: Sustainable, physics-compliant manufacturing.
